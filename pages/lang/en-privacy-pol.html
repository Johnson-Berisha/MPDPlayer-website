MPDPlayer is built as an Ad Supported App with an In-App-Purchase ro remove Ads and Unlock extra features like to control the Widget and the Watch App. Without this In-App-Purchase it is only possible to display the current playing song. This App is provided and developed by Adrian Berisha and can be contacted under: mail@mpdplayer.de.

The Advertising, provided by Google, collect Informations by an AD-ID (or known as IDFA = Identifier for Advertisers), to provide Ads that conforms to your Interests (Ad-Tracking). This can be deactivited in the Settings of your Device. To get more Information about the Privacy Policy of Google, visit: Google's Privacy Policy. Except for Google's data, no personal information is stored or shared.
